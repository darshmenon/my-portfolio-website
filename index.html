About Me

I am a passionate Robotics Software Developer currently working at Asimov Robotics, where I apply my expertise in developing cutting-edge software for autonomous systems. Prior to this, I gained valuable experience as a Software Developer at GrowthJockey, where I honed my coding skills and worked on complex software systems.
I graduated from BITS Pilani with a degree in Mechanical Engineering and am now excited to pursue my passion for robotics at Northeastern University in Boston. My primary interest lies in the intersection of robotics, AI, and human interaction. I am particularly passionate about autonomous navigation, drones, AI, machine learning, and deep learning.
My ultimate dream is to make humanoid robots accessible to everyone—not just as tools but as companions who can help improve the quality of life for people. Whether it's assisting with daily tasks, providing companionship, or helping individuals with disabilities, I believe humanoid robots have the potential to make a meaningful impact on society.
